Privacy Policy  ·  Last updated: June 2025

🔒

Our Commitment

KeySprintX is a fully offline typing trainer. We built it with a simple principle: your data stays on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or share any personal information — ever.

No accounts. No tracking. No ads. No internet required.
💾

Data Stored on Your Device

All data is stored locally using Android's SharedPreferences and never leaves your device:

  • Typing test results (WPM, accuracy, mistakes, timestamps)
  • App settings (test duration, difficulty, display preferences)
  • Per-letter mistake counts for the Statistics screen

You can delete all stored data at any time via Settings → Clear All History.

📡

No Internet Access

KeySprintX does not require an internet connection and makes no network requests. No data is sent to external servers, analytics platforms, or third-party services.

🤝

Third-Party Services

This app does not integrate any third-party SDKs for advertising, analytics, crash reporting, or social login. The only external dependency used at runtime is Google Fonts, which loads font files bundled within the app package — no runtime network calls are made.

👶

Children's Privacy

KeySprintX is suitable for all ages. Because we collect no personal information whatsoever, the app complies with COPPA and similar children's privacy regulations by design.

📋

Changes to This Policy

If we ever update this privacy policy, the revised version will be included in the next app update on Google Play. Continued use of the app after an update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
